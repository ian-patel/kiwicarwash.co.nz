About us

Kiwi car wash that provide the number one experience and environment for customers to clean their vehicles. Unlike all other competitors in the industry, on booking, we will come and meet you anywhere; whether it's your home, your office car park, on the street or even if your car is still in the showroom! And we provides every possible form of cleaning technology and service combined across Auckland.

We are currently experiencing a period of strong growth both domestically and nationally and whether as a valued client, or as part of the Kiwi Car Wash we look forward to you being a part of our future.

Quality

To be the best, we must have an ongoing commitment to quality in every aspect of our operations including:

  We will always use the best technology and materials in construction.

  We will always use the best car washing technologies available. Our emphasis is to use technology rather than 'muscle' to clean vehicles.

Value

Not only do people get the best service in the world but get it at a great price. To us, value is driven by:

  The price the customer pays for the service!

  The quality of the output the customers receive!

  How much time customers have to spend getting the service!

  How easy it is to access the site!

  How simple the technology is to use!

  How helpful the staff are!

Fun

We like to have fun! People want to call us back us because they have a great experience while we were there.

What makes a pleasant experience varies dependent upon the personality and needs of each individual customer. For example, some people love a friendly environment. Others love the result they get from the technology. Others love the ability to get their hands dirty and clean their vehicle themselves.

By catering for all of these and other needs, our customers have fun and want to call us back.

Eco Friendly

Kiwi Car Wash is a new waterless auto detailer who uses nothing but all natural eco-friendly products to clean your car, saving 50 gallons of water per car. That's a lot of water to save! Our products are completely non-toxic and smell clean.
